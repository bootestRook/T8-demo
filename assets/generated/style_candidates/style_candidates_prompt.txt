Each candidate should show one key scene with a survivor hero, zombie horde pressure, ruined urban shelter or street battlefield, 3 to 5 large readable cards at the bottom, and an energy bar visual hint. Candidate A: gritty wasteland comic realism, dusty amber sky, cracked asphalt, improvised barricades, tactical survivor with flashlight and worn jacket, zombies as strong silhouettes, card frames made of scratched metal and hazard tape.

Candidate B: stylized readable cartoon apocalypse, chunky shapes, saturated teal-orange lighting, expressive survivor squad, exaggerated zombies, bold thick outlines, large simple card icons and friendly mobile UI.

Candidate C: neon quarantine zone, dark blue and acid green palette, glowing biohazard signs, rain-soaked street, masked survivor, infected zombies with rim light, sleek glass-and-warning-stripe card UI.
